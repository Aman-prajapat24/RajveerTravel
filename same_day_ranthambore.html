Two Day Jaipur Sightseeing Tour

Overview

Embark on an exhilarating journey from Jaipur to Ranthambore National Park, offering a thrilling opportunity to witness the regal Bengal Tiger in its natural habitat. This private day trip is a wildlife enthusiast's dream, providing a close encounter with not only tigers but also a diverse range of wildlife, including leopards, bears, langur monkeys, hyenas, deer, crocodiles, and various bird species.

About Ranthambore

Ranthambore National Park, situated in the Sawai Madhopur district, approximately 180 km from Jaipur, was once the royal hunting grounds of the Jaipur royal family. The park is not only a wildlife sanctuary but also a historical treasure, boasting a majestic fort and ancient temples.

Tour Details

Tour Duration: Approximately 14-15 hours

Tour Type: Private Tour

Transport: Pickup and drop-off are available from any hotel within Jaipur city limits.

Jungle Safari: Choose between a jeep or a canter for your safari.

Itinerary

08:30 am: Jaipur – Ranthambore (Approx. 180 km, 3 hrs)
Begin your Ranthambore adventure at 8:30 am, departing from Jaipur after a hearty breakfast. Our driver will pick you up from your chosen location, whether it's your hotel or the airport in Jaipur, and transport you in an air-conditioned car to Ranthambore.

12:30 pm: Ranthambore National Park
Arrive at Ranthambore National Park around 12:30 pm and prepare for an exciting jungle safari aboard a Gypsy or Canter vehicle. Please note that there will be a standard identification process before entering the park, so ensure you have your original photo ID with you.

3-Hour Jungle Safari:
Your jungle safari in Ranthambore National Park will last for approximately 3 hours. Enjoy the thrill of exploring wildlife in their natural habitat within your allotted forest area. The safari is conducted in an open-top 6-seater jeep or a 20-seater open-top canter, guided by a government-approved naturalist.

06:00 pm: Return to Jaipur
Your jungle safari concludes around 5:30 pm or 6:00 pm, and after your Ranthambore National Park exploration, your driver will transport you back to Jaipur. You can request a dinner stop on the way if you wish.

09:00 pm: Arrival in Jaipur
Expect to arrive in Jaipur around 9:00 pm or 9:30 pm. Our driver will drop you off at your hotel or your preferred location.

Tour Summary

This unforgettable wildlife expedition to Ranthambore National Park promises to etch remarkable memories that will last for years to come. Witness the untamed beauty of nature in its purest form on this captivating journey.
